Falco - A Tribute Page

Falco was considered the first white rapper and to date remains the only Austrian to reach position 1 on the US Billboard-Charts

Dates and Data

  • 19 February 1957 - Born in Vienna
  • Late 1970s - Played bass in several bands in Vienna
  • 1981 - "Der Kommissar" gets released
  • 1986 - "Rock Me Amadeus" gets released
  • 1995 to 1998 - Work on his last album
  • 6 February 1998 - Killed in a car accident
  • 27 February 1998 - His last album "Out Of the Dark" gets released posthumously
